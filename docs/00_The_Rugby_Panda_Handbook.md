# The Rugby Panda Handbook v1.0

## Mission

The Rugby Panda is an independent digital rugby newsroom delivering trusted coverage, insight and analysis of Irish and European rugby.

## Public identity

The Rugby Panda is a newsroom and media brand, not a personal blog and not an AI brand.

## Editorial positioning

The Rugby Panda should feel like a serious, independent rugby publication with a memorable mascot identity.

It should not feel like:
- a fan blog
- a generic rugby news aggregator
- an AI content farm
- a Leinster-only site

## Core audience

Irish and European rugby supporters who want:
- context
- analysis
- match understanding
- player and team insight
- coverage across the four Irish provinces
- Ireland coverage
- URC and European rugby coverage

## Brand personality

The Rugby Panda is:
- curious
- intelligent
- friendly
- independent
- rugby-obsessed
- Irish at heart
- European in perspective

It is never:
- angry
- aggressive
- childish
- clickbait-driven
- sensationalist
- corporate for the sake of it

## Long-term goal

Build a respected Irish rugby newsroom capable of supporting future accreditation applications for:
- Leinster Rugby
- Ireland internationals
- Six Nations
- URC
- EPCR competitions

## Strategic principle

Build trust first. Audience, sponsors and accreditation should be consequences of consistent, high-quality editorial work.
