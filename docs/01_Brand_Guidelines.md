# Brand Guidelines

## Name

The Rugby Panda

## Mission statement

Digital rugby newsroom delivering independent coverage, insight and analysis of Irish and European rugby.

## Logo direction

The final chosen direction is a friendly, memorable panda mascot with a green swoosh.

The panda should feel:
- wearable
- sticker-worthy
- friendly
- curious
- modern
- recognisable at small sizes

The panda should not feel:
- angry
- old
- sad
- corporate
- generic
- overly realistic

## Visual direction

The site should feel:
- premium
- clean
- editorial
- modern sports media
- highly readable
- not cluttered

Avoid:
- excessive ads
- noisy layouts
- generic templates
- fan-blog aesthetics
