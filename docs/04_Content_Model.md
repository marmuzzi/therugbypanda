# Content Model

## Core principle

Articles have one primary category and multiple tags.

## Primary categories

Navigation-level categories:

```text
Home
Provinces
Ireland
URC
Europe
About
```

## Province pages

Under Provinces:

```text
Leinster
Munster
Ulster
Connacht
```

Do not create Academy as a dedicated section initially. Academy stories should live under the relevant province and be tagged `Academy`.

## Europe

Europe includes:
- Champions Cup
- Challenge Cup
- EPCR news
- relevant French/English/European rugby stories

Champions Cup and Challenge Cup are not top-level nav items initially because Challenge Cup volume may be too low to sustain a fresh standalone section.

## Tags

Articles can have multiple tags.

Example tags:
- Leinster
- Munster
- Ulster
- Connacht
- Ireland
- URC
- Champions Cup
- Challenge Cup
- Six Nations
- Autumn Nations
- Summer Tour
- Transfer
- Injury
- Academy
- Contract
- Selection
- Analysis
- Opinion
- Tactical Breakdown
- Player Ratings

## Entities

Future entity types:

```text
Player
Coach
Team
Province
Competition
Venue
```

## Search

Search should eventually search across:
- articles
- players
- coaches
- provinces
- teams
- competitions
- tags

Search should be available in the site header.

Suggested placeholder:
`Search players, clubs or competitions...`
