# Decisions Log

## Decision 001 — Brand name

Chosen name:
The Rugby Panda

Reason:
Memorable, authentic, connected to Ageing Pandas rugby camaraderie, and distinctive as a media brand.

## Decision 002 — Public mission

Use:
Digital rugby newsroom delivering independent coverage, insight and analysis of Irish and European rugby.

## Decision 003 — No public AI positioning

AI is an internal tool only.

Do not position the brand publicly as AI-generated or AI-powered.

Reason:
In Ireland, AI may be seen as shortcut rather than a professional tool.

## Decision 004 — Article attribution

All articles are signed:
The Rugby Panda

Reason:
Brand-first newsroom. Trust belongs to the publication.

## Decision 005 — No personal author profile initially

Do not use Matteo's name as article author.

Reason:
The Rugby Panda should not feel like a personal blog.

## Decision 006 — Navigation simplicity

Initial navigation:
Home, Provinces, Ireland, URC, Europe, About

Reason:
Avoid empty sections and keep the site manageable.

## Decision 007 — Provinces must include all four Irish provinces

The Rugby Panda should cover:
Leinster, Munster, Ulster, Connacht

Reason:
If we say Irish rugby, we must not appear Leinster-only.

## Decision 008 — No dedicated Academy section initially

Academy stories live under province pages and use tags.

Reason:
Academy coverage volume will likely be inconsistent.

## Decision 009 — Europe instead of Champions Cup / Challenge Cup top-level nav

Europe includes Champions Cup and Challenge Cup.

Reason:
Challenge Cup may not sustain regular content by itself.

## Decision 010 — Opinion is a content type

Do not create top-level Opinion initially.

Reason:
Opinion belongs under the relevant subject.

## Decision 011 — One primary category, many tags

Each article has one primary category and multiple tags.

Reason:
Clean navigation with flexible discovery.

## Decision 012 — Search is essential

Search should eventually cover players, clubs, competitions, tags and articles.

Reason:
Readers may arrive looking for specific subjects such as Sam Prendergast.

## Decision 013 — Reader-first sponsorship

Advertising must never interrupt reading.

Reason:
Trust and readability are more important than low-value ad clutter.

## Decision 014 — Start newsroom build with article page

The article page is the core product.

Reason:
Most traffic from Google/social lands on articles.
